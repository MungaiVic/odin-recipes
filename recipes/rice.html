How to cook rice

Desciption

This is the basmati rice for you, just like in the restaurants

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups water
  • 1 cup of basmati rice
  • ¼ cups frozen green peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

Steps

In a saucepan bring water to a boil. Add rice and stir. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.

When rice is cooked, stir in peas and cumin. Cover and let stand for 5 minutes. After that, it will be ready.
